  • You may enable an optional biometric lock (Face ID / Touch ID) that requires authentication before displaying intimacy data.
  • You are solely responsible for the physical security of your device and the data stored on it.
Not medical

Intimacy logs are not a form of contraception, STI prevention, or medical family planning. Capsule does not assist in sexual health decisions; consult a qualified healthcare professional.

06 Apple Health Integration

When you enable Health sync, Capsule reads (but never writes) the following data from Apple Health: sleep hours, step count, and resting heart rate. Health data is used only to display alongside your daily log in the App, and may optionally be shared with the AI service if you have granted AI consent.

You may revoke Health access at any time through the iOS Settings app (Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Capsule) or within Capsule's Settings.

07 Partner Mode

Capsule includes an optional Partner Mode, which allows you to share a limited read-only summary of your cycle with a partner who has their own Capsule account. Partner Mode shares:

  • Current cycle phase
  • Upcoming period prediction
  • A selected mood snapshot

Partner Mode does not share intimacy logs, free-form journal notes, medication records, or detailed symptom data. You may disconnect Partner Mode at any time from Settings, which immediately revokes your partner's access.
