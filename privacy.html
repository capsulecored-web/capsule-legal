Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Capsule — AI Cycle Tracker ("Capsule", "the App", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

1. Data We Collect

Capsule stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cycle data: menstrual period start/end dates, cycle length, period duration.
  • Daily logs: symptoms (mood, pain level, energy, physical and emotional symptoms), flow intensity, notes.
  • Vitamins/medications: supplement names, schedules, and completion status.
  • Preferences: language, theme, notification settings.

If you create an account (optional), we also collect:

  • Authentication data: Apple ID token (via Sign in with Apple). We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.
  • Nickname: a display name you choose within the App.
  • Profile photo: if you upload one (stored in Supabase cloud storage).

We do not collect your real name, phone number, physical address, or financial information.

2. How Data Is Stored

All cycle and symptom data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. If you sign in, your data is also synced to a cloud database powered by Supabase (supabase.com) to enable cross-device access and backup. Supabase stores data in secure, encrypted infrastructure.

3. Third-Party AI Service — Data Sharing

Important: Capsule uses a third-party AI service to provide personalized wellness tips. Data is only shared with this service after you give explicit consent within the App.

Who receives data: Capsule sends data to Claude AI, a large language model developed by Anthropic, PBC (anthropic.com). Anthropic is a separate, independent company and is not affiliated with Capsule.

What data is sent:

  • Current cycle phase (menstrual, follicular, ovulation, luteal)
  • Current cycle day (numeric value)
  • Average cycle length and average period duration
  • Symptoms you have logged (mood, pain, energy, and other tracked symptoms)

What is NOT sent:

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Your age, location, or any device identifiers
  • Your profile photo
  • Raw calendar dates or historical cycle dates

Purpose of data sharing: The data is sent solely to generate a personalized AI-powered wellness response (e.g. cycle-phase advice, symptom insights) in real time.

Data retention by Anthropic: Anthropic processes Capsule's requests under a Zero Data Retention policy. This means your data is not stored on Anthropic's servers after the response is generated and is not used to train or improve AI models.

Your consent and control: The App requests your explicit permission before any data is sent to the AI service. You may decline, and the App will function normally without AI features. You can revoke your consent at any time in the App's Settings. Once revoked, no further data will be sent to the AI service.

4. Third-Party Services Summary

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): AI wellness tips generation. Data shared: cycle phase, cycle day, symptoms. Retention: Zero Data Retention.
  • Supabase: Cloud authentication and data sync (optional, only if signed in). Data stored: cycle data, symptoms, profile.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. Data shared: anonymous app user ID, purchase receipts. No health data is shared with RevenueCat.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple): Authentication only. We receive an identity token; Apple controls what information (email, name) is shared with us.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures: encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure cloud storage via Supabase, and no plaintext storage of sensitive tokens. All AI requests are transmitted over encrypted connections.

6. Children's Privacy

Capsule is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data Capsule stores about you
  • Delete your account and all associated cloud data (available in Settings)
  • Revoke AI consent at any time (available in Settings)
  • Export your cycle data (PDF export available in the App)
  • Use the App without creating an account (all data stays on-device)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
