Capsule — AI Cycle Tracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Capsule is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used and stored, and with whom it is shared.

Capsule tracks sensitive health information — including menstrual cycle data and, where you choose to log it, information about sexual activity. We treat all such data with particular care. You remain in full control of what is recorded and where it is stored.

01 Data We Collect

Capsule stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Cycle data: menstrual period start/end dates, cycle length, period duration, predicted and confirmed ovulation.
  • Daily logs: symptoms (mood, pain level, energy, physical and emotional symptoms), flow intensity, notes, free-form journal entries.
  • Intimacy logs (optional): when enabled by you, records of sexual activity, with categories you may select — protected, unprotected, with a partner, or solo. These entries are tied to a specific date.
  • Apple Health data (read-only, optional): when you enable Health sync, the App reads sleep hours, step count, and resting heart rate from Apple Health to display alongside your daily log. Health data is read only on demand and never written back to Apple Health or transmitted off your device except as described in Section 04.
  • Vitamins/medications: supplement names, schedules, and completion status.
  • Preferences: language, theme, notification settings, intimacy-lock setting.

If you create an account

  • Authentication data: Apple ID token (via Sign in with Apple) or email address. We do not receive or store your Apple ID password.
  • Nickname: a display name you choose within the App.
  • Profile photo: if you upload one (stored in Supabase cloud storage).
  • Partner link (optional): if you use Partner Mode to share your cycle with a partner, a pairing code links two accounts. Your partner can view a limited read-only summary of your cycle. Intimacy logs and free-form notes are never shared with the partner.
What we never collect

Your real name, phone number, physical address, financial information, location, or device identifiers.

02 How Data Is Stored

All cycle, symptom, and intimacy data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

If you sign in, your data is also synced to a cloud database powered by Supabase, hosted in the European Union. Supabase stores data in secure, encrypted infrastructure with TLS in transit and AES-256 at rest.

If you use the App without an account, no data ever leaves your device — with the sole exception of AI requests described in Section 04, which are only sent after your explicit consent.

03 Biometric Lock for Intimacy Data

Capsule offers an optional Intimacy Lock feature that requires Face ID or Touch ID authentication before displaying intimacy log details on the calendar or in day view.

  • Biometric authentication is performed entirely on your device by iOS.
  • Capsule never receives, processes, or stores your biometric data (face scan, fingerprint).
  • We only receive a success or failure signal from iOS, which we use to unlock the intimacy view within the current session.
  • Disabling the lock is available in Settings at any time.

04 Third-Party AI Service

Important

Capsule uses a third-party AI service to provide personalized wellness tips. Data is only shared with this service after you give explicit consent within the App.

Who receives data

Capsule sends data to Claude AI, a large language model developed by Anthropic, PBC (anthropic.com). Anthropic is a separate, independent company and is not affiliated with Capsule.

What data is sent

  • Current cycle phase (menstrual, follicular, ovulation, luteal)
  • Current cycle day (numeric value)
  • Average cycle length and average period duration
  • Symptoms you have logged for the current day
  • Recent Apple Health metrics (sleep, steps, resting heart rate) — only if you have enabled Health sync

What is NOT sent

  • Your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Your age, location, or any device identifiers
  • Your profile photo
  • Raw calendar dates or historical cycle dates
  • Intimacy logs of any kind — information about sexual activity is never transmitted to the AI service under any circumstance.
  • Free-form journal notes
  • Partner-mode data

Purpose

The data is sent solely to generate a personalized AI-powered wellness response (cycle-phase advice, symptom insights) in real time.

Retention by Anthropic

Anthropic processes Capsule's requests under a Zero Data Retention policy. Your data is not stored on Anthropic's servers after the response is generated and is not used to train or improve AI models.

Your consent and control

The App requests your explicit permission before any data is sent to the AI service. You may decline — the App will function normally without AI features. You can revoke your consent at any time in Settings. Once revoked, no further data will be sent.

05 Third-Party Services Summary

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): AI wellness tips. Data shared: cycle phase, day, symptoms, optional Health metrics. Intimacy logs and notes are never shared. Retention: Zero Data Retention.
  • Supabase (EU region): Cloud authentication and data sync (optional). Data stored: cycle data, symptoms, intimacy logs (encrypted at rest), profile, partner link.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. Data shared: anonymous app user ID, purchase receipts. No health or intimacy data is shared.
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple): Authentication only. We receive an identity token; Apple controls what information is shared with us.
  • Apple HealthKit: Read-only access to sleep, steps, and resting heart rate when you explicitly enable it. Data stays on your device except as described in Section 04.

06 Analytics & Tracking

Capsule does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or user tracking of any kind. We do not build usage profiles, we do not track you across apps, and we do not share data with advertisers. The App does not use cookies.

07 Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures: encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted-at-rest cloud storage via Supabase (EU region), biometric device-level protection for intimacy data (if enabled by you), and no plaintext storage of sensitive tokens. All AI requests are transmitted over encrypted connections.

08 Children's Privacy

Capsule is not intended for children below the minimum age required by your local jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals below this age. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

09 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data Capsule stores about you.
  • Delete your account and all associated cloud data (Settings → "Delete Account").
  • Export your cycle data to PDF (Settings → "Export Data").
  • Revoke AI consent at any time in Settings.
  • Disable Health sync, Intimacy Lock, or Partner Mode at any time in Settings.
  • Use the App without an account — all data stays on-device.

Users located in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the United Kingdom have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK Data Protection Act, including rectification, restriction of processing, portability, and objection to processing. Users in California have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

10 Data Retention

If you delete your account, your cloud data is permanently removed within 30 days. Local on-device data is removed immediately upon App deletion. AI request data is not retained by Anthropic (see Section 04).

11 International Transfers

If you are located outside the European Economic Area, your data may be transferred to and stored in Supabase's EU infrastructure. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the appropriate legal mechanisms to protect such transfers.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated to you in the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
